COVID-19 Dashboard

Instructions

The map can be dragged and zoomed to find countries of interest. The colours and circles on the map represent the most recent values of COVID-19 cases per million population in each country.

Clicking a country on the map will update the line charts to show that country's data. The attribute shown on each line chart can be selected from the available dropdowns — and the horizontal axis of all charts can be altered similarly.

Finally, scatter plots are available to compare the effect of COVID-19 on a country's population with country summary statistics. Points are coloured by k-means clustering analysis, and can be highlighted across the plots for comparison by clicking and dragging a box aroud them.
