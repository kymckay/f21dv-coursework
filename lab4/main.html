Frequency of initials

You may notice that initials in the set of least popular names tend to start with letters towards the end of the alphabet. This may be unsurprising since chapter lengths in any English dictonary would suggest they're less common leading letters. However, it does prompt a question: which letters are the most common initials and how does the distribution change through the years?
